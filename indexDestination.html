🌸 Durban Cultural Half-Day Tour

A journey through the city's rich tapestry

📍 Depart from Umhlanga / Durban Hotel

Enjoy a scenic drive along the coastline into the vibrant heart of Durban City.

📍 Durban Natural Science Museum

Located in the historic City Hall, this fascinating museum showcases natural history exhibits.

📍 KwaMuhle Museum

Gain insight into Durban's social and political past during the apartheid era.

📍 Early Morning Market

Fresh produce, spices, flowers, and the rhythm of local life.

📍 Muthi Market

Traditional African medicine shops with herbs, roots, and potions.

📍 Victoria Street Market

Handcrafted souvenirs, colourful fabrics, and aromatic spice stalls.

📍 Drive via Greyville Racecourse

Home of the annual Durban July.

📍 Lunch at The Oriental Restaurant

Durban-style curry, naan, and local Indian heritage flavours.

📍 Return to Umhlanga / Durban Hotel

Scenic drive back along the Golden Mile, optional photo stop.
