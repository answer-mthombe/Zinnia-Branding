About Us

Zinnia Holdings

Empowering Brands. Enriching Lives.

Zinnia Holdings is a purpose-driven, faith-aligned holding company committed to building sustainable businesses that empower individuals, families, and communities. Rooted in excellence, integrity, and innovation, we steward brands that operate across wellness, branding, events, hospitality, and destination services.

Our ecosystem is designed to serve people holistically — nurturing health, creativity, celebration, experience, and economic empowerment - while honoring God’s faithfulness and demonstrating His excellence in all we do.

Our Vision

To create a dynamic business ecosystem that uplifts lives, creates opportunities, and delivers world-class solutions across multiple industries.

Our Mission

To build, support, and scale impactful brands that serve with purpose, professionalism, and compassion — empowering brands while enriching lives.
