Our Story

Since 2015

Zinnia Destination Management was born from a passion for showcasing the authentic beauty, culture, and hospitality of KwaZulu-Natal. What started as a small local tour operation has grown into a trusted travel partner for individuals, families, and corporate groups seeking meaningful experiences.

Our name, Zinnia, symbolizes endurance, daily remembrance, and lasting friendship — values we embed in every journey we craft. We believe travel should be more than just sightseeing; it should be transformative, connecting you with the heart of a place and its people.
