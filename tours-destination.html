🌸 Durban Cultural Half-Day Tour

A journey through the city's rich tapestry

📍 Depart from Umhlanga / Durban Hotel

Enjoy a scenic drive along the coastline into the vibrant heart of Durban City.

📍 Durban Natural Science Museum

Located in the historic City Hall, this fascinating museum showcases natural history exhibits.

📍 KwaMuhle Museum

Gain insight into Durban's social and political past during the apartheid era.

📍 Early Morning Market

Fresh produce, spices, flowers, and the rhythm of local life.

📍 Muthi Market

Traditional African medicine shops with herbs, roots, and potions.

📍 Victoria Street Market

Handcrafted souvenirs, colourful fabrics, and aromatic spice stalls.

📍 Drive via Greyville Racecourse

Home of the annual Durban July.

📍 Lunch at The Oriental Restaurant

Durban-style curry, naan, and local Indian heritage flavours.

📍 Return to Umhlanga / Durban Hotel

Scenic drive back along the Golden Mile, optional photo stop.

Tour Includes

  • Private, air-conditioned vehicle
  • Professional local guide
  • Bottled water on board
  • Hotel pickup and drop-off

Tour Information

Duration: Approximately 4-5 hours

Departure: 9:00 AM from your hotel

Price: Available on request

🌼 Inanda Township Heritage & Culture Tour

Half Day Tour

Starting Point: Mahatma Gandhi Phoenix Settlement

Discover the vibrant heart of Inanda - a township that tells South Africa's story of struggle, resilience, and unity. This guided cultural experience explores historic landmarks, celebrates local heritage, and offers a taste of authentic township life and cuisine.

Highlights:
  • Mahatma Gandhi Phoenix Settlement
  • Ohlange Institute (Dr. John Langalibalele Dube's home)
  • Inanda Heritage Route
  • Local community interaction
  • Traditional lunch option available
Tour Details:
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Includes: Guide, transport, water
  • Optional: Lunch at local restaurant
